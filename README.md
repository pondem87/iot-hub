# Pfitztronics IoT Hub

## 1. Product Vision
To provide affordable, reliable, and transparent monitoring and control for critical water and power infrastructure — across both single-owner and shared, multi-tenant systems.

Key principles:
- Designed for unattended, remote environments
- Operates under poor connectivity and unreliable power
- Simple for farmers and caretakers
- Powerful enough for technicians, engineers, and managers
- Cloud-first, but not cloud-dependent for safe operation
- Built-in auditability and traceability
